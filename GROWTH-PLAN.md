# Easy Chat Maker — Growth & SEO Plan

Goal: grow easychatmaker.com traffic and rank top 10 for fake-chat / chat-mockup /
chat-story-video keywords.

Working agreement: items marked **[Claude]** are done in Claude sessions (code,
pages, copy). Items marked **[You]** need a human — accounts, posting, outreach,
recording. Check items off as they're done.

## Market snapshot (July 2026)

- Competitors ranking today (fakedetail.com, zeoob.com, fakewhats.com,
  prankshit.com, ifaketextmessage.com) all win with **one dedicated page per
  keyword** — old domains, lots of pages, mediocre products.
- **Soft spots**: AI-chat mockups (ChatGPT contested by small players; Claude
  and Gemini almost uncontested) and **chat story VIDEO makers** (TikTok
  creator niche — convoclip.com, aicut.pro; most fake-chat sites have no video).
- **Our edge**: free + no watermark + video export + AI platforms, all in one.
- Realistic timelines: long-tail (Claude/Gemini/AI mockup) top-10 in 1–3
  months; per-platform generators 3–6 months; "fake chat generator" head term
  6–12 months. New domain = links matter as much as pages.
